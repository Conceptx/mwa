Our Services

We are a fully integrated loan servicing company, with services that span across the full life-cycle of the loan: from loan origination through pay off. This includes:.

  • Collecting monthly payments from the borrower
  • Sending the lender monthly statements
  • Maintaining records of payments and balances
  • Collecting and paying taxes
  • Paying insurance
  • Managing escrow and impound funds
  • Preventing loan delinquency
  • Managing delinquent loans

    • Our Loan Servicing Process

    Once investors contact us with a loan that needs to be serviced:

  • We contact the Borrower and provide them with all the necessary information to make payments
  • We offer Borrowers a wide range of payment options, from ACH (Direct Deposit), to checks, phone and online payments
  • We provide ongoing customer service to the borrower so our Lenders can focus on their core activities
  • Once we receive payment from the Borrower, we send the Lender the payment along with a monthly statement and other report
  • We originate, manage and keep all loan documentation, including statements, insurance documentation, etc.
